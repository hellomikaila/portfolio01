Sporkula

  • Released : February 26, 2015
  • Categories: Ideation, Film Production, Prototyping

We were asked to create a new product and showcase it in a short film. Meet Sporkula. Sporkula is what the spork achieves when it graduates mediocrity… We see other disposable utensils as a waste of our time. We prototyped and 3D-printed a new type of disposable utensil, one with a knife that detaches so that you can actually use it to cut. No knife, no deal.It also uses less material than the traditional fork, spoon and knife trio. Check it out on TinkerCAD. This was shot, chopped and scored by myself and Christina Foy (XD, 2016).
